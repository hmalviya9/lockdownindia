Same discipline as a forensic dossier. Six escalation triggers, three structural defenses, secondary signals. Each input traces to a primary or tier-1 secondary source. Manual overrides remain visible in the audit trail. The probability is a structured judgment, not a clinical measurement.

What the dial measures

A composite probability that India will impose a COVID-style nationwide lockdown within the next 30 days, driven by oil-supply shock from the US-Iran war. Six escalation triggers carry positive weight; structural defenses (strategic reserves, import diversification, fiscal absorption) carry implicit negative weight via the trigger thresholds.

The four bands

GREEN (0–14%) — status quo, no formal restrictions.
YELLOW (15–34%) — targeted measures probable: LPG quotas, work-from-home advisories, weekend curbs.
ORANGE (35–59%) — state-level energy emergencies likely; aviation fuel curbs.
RED (60–100%) — full nationwide lockdown probable within 30 days.

Honesty caveats

This is a decision-support tool, not a prediction guarantee. The keyword scanner will sometimes mis-classify; manual review is required. Treat it as a faster pair of eyes, not an oracle. A single 6-hour reading is noise — the value is the trend.
