09 · Methodology

How the dial works.

Same discipline as the bryanjohnson v3 dossier. Every input is sourced. The scoring function is auditable. Manual overrides are visible in state.json.

Scoring weights (max 125, normalized to 100)

  • T1Strait of Hormuz status — open / restricted / closed days. Max 20 points.
  • T2Iran strikes on Saudi/UAE oil infra — none / threats / confirmed / sustained. Max 20 points.
  • T3Brent crude price — <$90 / $90-110 / $110-130 / $130-150 sustained / >$150. Max 15 points.
  • T4US ground escalation — none / buildup / kinetic land / open ground war. Max 15 points.
  • T5India SPR days remaining — >60 / 45-60 / 30-45 / <30. Max 15 points.
  • T6Indian refinery attack — none / threats / attempted / successful. Max 30 points (heaviest single weight).
  • +SSecondary signals — government denial language, LPG cylinder delays, INR-USD stress, regional escalation. Net ±10 points.

Verdict bands

  • 0–15GREEN · Status quo, no formal restrictions.
  • 15–35YELLOW · Targeted measures probable (LPG quotas, WFH advisories, weekend movement curbs).
  • 35–60ORANGE · State-level energy emergencies likely; aviation fuel curbs.
  • 60–100RED · Full nationwide lockdown probable within 30 days.

Update cadence

  • ⏱Every 6 hours · launchd job runs scripts/update-indicator.sh · fetches RSS feeds, parses keywords, recalculates score, appends to history.jsonl.
  • 📡Auto-refresh · this page polls state.json every 60 seconds and re-renders if the indicator has changed.
  • ⚠Manual override · edit state.json directly when human judgment must override the script (e.g. breaking news the keyword scanner missed).
